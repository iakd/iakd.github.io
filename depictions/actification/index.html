Actification

Supports iOS 8 and 9

Creating custom notifications:
After you installed the tweak, a folder "Actification" will be created in "/var/mobile/" on you iDevice. All your custom notifications will be placed in it.

  • Navigate to "/var/mobile/Actification/" using your favorite filebrowser (on iDevice: e.g. Filza).
  • You will see an existing example notification file called "Example.plist". Duplicate this file and give the copy a useful name, but keep the .plist ending (e.g. MyFirstNotification.plist).
  • Now open your new notification file with either a plist-editor or text-editor. The file basically contains three sections/keys with simple text values you can edit:
    • NotificationTitle: specifies the headline text of your notification (in the example the headline is "Example Notification")
    NotificationContent: specifies the body text of your notification (in the example the body text is "This is an example message sent with Actification")
    NotificationOrigin: specifies the icon of your notification (I would recommend to keep "com.apple.MobileSMS" like in the example)
  • After you saved your new notification just respring/reboot your iDevice and the notifications will be assignable in Activator.
